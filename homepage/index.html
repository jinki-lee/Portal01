How it works

All ENMAX IT Full Time Employees will be given 200 Give Points every month.

Team leads and above will be given 1000 Give Points every month.

Give Points can be converted to ENMAX Points which can be used to purchase reward items.


ENMAX contracters can also be recognized, but cannot receive Give Points.

Give us a feedback

How do I get more Give Points?

  1. At the end of every month, employees will be automatically given 200 Give Points.
  2. Everytime you recognize and reward someone, you will also be given 10 Give Points as well!
  3. The Multiplier Effect:

    Continuously rewarding at least one other employee every week will increase your Multiplier and Give Points ammount next month.

    Week Multiplier Next Month Top Off
    1 x 1.00 200 GP
    2 x 1.25 250 GP
    3 x 1.50 300 GP
    4 x 2.00 400 GP

    You give and you get!
